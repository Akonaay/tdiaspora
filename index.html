Tanzania Diaspora Initiative (TDI) - A Response to the needs of the Tanzanias in the Diaspora!

Until the recent past, the recounts of the most Tanzanians in the Diaspora had been filled with horrific and disappointing experiences regarding mismanagememnt of funds and investment attempts back home in Tanzania

Most Diasporas entrusted their projects and investments back home to their immediate relations, which more often than not led to devasting consequences. Some Diasporas even far the thought of returning home with little to show for their stay abroad.
