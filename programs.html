Some of the TDI Objectives

Prepare, organize and disseminate relevant information via all available mediums on development, investment, volunteer opportunities and all means of engagement.

Utilization of Diasporas as resource by the host countries, the role of Diasporas in development and nation building and the impacts of globalization on diasporic networks, organizations and movements.


Coordinate awareness courses for Diaspora residing in or traveling to the Middle East and other countries for Employment, on the culture of the country, on the rules governing employment, their rights, and available resources and recourse.
